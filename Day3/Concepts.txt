2 Identifiers - names given to variables, functions, classes, etc.
Rules for identifiers:
    1. Can contain letters, digits, and underscores
    2. Cannot start with a digit
    3. Cannot be a keyword
    4. Case-sensitive
    5. Cannot contain special characters (except underscore)
    6. There no specific length limit for writting variable
